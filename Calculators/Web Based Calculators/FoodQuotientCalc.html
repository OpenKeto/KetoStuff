RQ Values

  • RQ of 0.7 = Fat being oxidized
  • RQ of 0.8 = Protein being oxidized
  • RQ of 0.85 = Equal amount of fat and carbohydrates being oxidized
  • RQ of 1.0 = Carbohydrates being oxidized
  • The respiratory quotient (RQ) is closer to the food quotient (FQ) for subjects eating high-fat diets than it is for subjects eating high-carbohydrate diets.
